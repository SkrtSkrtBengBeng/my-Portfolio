Collection


            Collecting things has always been a passion of mine. Whether 
			it's rare coins, vintage toys, or unique artifacts, each item 
			tells a story. My collection started small but has grown over 
			the years, reflecting my interests and adventures.
            One of my favorite pieces is an old compass from a family heirloom. 
			It reminds me of my travels and the sense of exploration. Collecting 
			isn't just about owning things; it's about preserving memories and 
			learning history.
            I also collect books on medieval history, which ties into my love for 
			fantasy and ancient tales. Each book adds to my knowledge and inspires 
			my creative projects. If you're into collecting, remember: it's the 
			journey that matters most.
